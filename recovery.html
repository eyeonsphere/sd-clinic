Questions

Frequently asked questions

Who is Revel Health?
Revel Health connects members directly with board-certified physicians who prescribe clinician-grade GLP-1, NAD+, Sermorelin, and longevity therapies online — no waiting rooms, no insurance required. A provider reviews every profile before anything ships, and if a program isn't the right fit, you get a full refund.
How does it work?
Choose a program and complete checkout, finish a short online intake covering your health history and goals, then a licensed provider reviews your information and builds a plan. Once approved, your treatment ships directly from a licensed pharmacy.
What's included in the price?
Every program bundles the secure intake, provider review, medication (if approved), and discreet delivery — pricing stays the same regardless of dosage, with monthly or one-time options available.
What happens if I'm not approved?
You're notified and refunded in full, and any subscription is canceled automatically — there are no ongoing charges and no risk in getting started.
How long does delivery take?
Once your intake is approved, orders are processed and shipped within 3–5 business days, with tracking sent as soon as the package is on its way.
Do I need insurance?
No — Revel Health is designed to work entirely without insurance. Pricing is transparent and paid out of pocket, with no dosage-based markups.
